I’m an AI Engineer at Rabobank, with an interest in the intersection of AI and Linguistics. I earned a Research MA in Computational Linguistics at Utrecht University in 2024.

In the past, I was an AI & Data Science Intern at TNO and Data Engineering Research Assistant for the Centre for Digital Humanities at Utrecht University. I have also worked as Data Analysis Research Assistant for CLARIAH and the PAMLab at Boston University, during my stay as a Fulbright scholar. My main areas of interest are: Natural Language Processing, Generative AI, Conversational AI and Machine Learning.
